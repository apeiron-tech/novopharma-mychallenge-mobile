# Privacy Policy for Novopharma Rewards App

**Last Updated:** [Current Date]  
**Effective Date:** [Current Date]

## 1. Introduction

Novopharma Rewards ("the App") is a gamified rewards and engagement platform designed for pharmacy employees. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

By downloading, installing, or using the Novopharma Rewards App, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
We collect the following personal information when you create an account:
- **Full Name**: Your complete legal name
- **Email Address**: For account authentication and communications
- **Phone Number**: Optional, for account recovery and important notifications
- **Date of Birth**: Optional, for age verification and account management
- **Profile Picture**: Optional avatar image uploaded by you
- **Position/Role**: Your job position within the pharmacy
- **City**: Your work location city
- **Pharmacy Information**: Pharmacy ID and name where you work

### 2.2 Authentication Data
- **Email and Password**: For secure account access
- **Device Authentication Tokens**: For secure session management

### 2.3 Usage and Performance Data
- **Points and Rewards**: Your accumulated points, pending points, and redeemed rewards
- **Sales Data**: Product sales you log through barcode scanning
- **Quiz Participation**: Quiz answers and scores for educational purposes
- **Goal Progress**: Your progress towards sales and engagement goals
- **Leaderboard Rankings**: Your competitive rankings and performance metrics
- **Activity History**: Timestamps of your app usage and interactions

### 2.4 Technical Data
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **App Usage Data**: Features used, time spent in app, crash reports
- **Network Information**: IP address, connection type for analytics and security

### 2.5 Camera and Image Data
- **Profile Images**: When you choose to upload or update your profile picture
- **Barcode Scanning**: Camera access for scanning product barcodes

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Purposes
- **Account Management**: To create, authenticate, and manage your user account
- **Rewards Program**: To track points, manage rewards, and facilitate redemptions
- **Performance Tracking**: To monitor sales progress, quiz performance, and goal achievement
- **Leaderboard Functionality**: To display competitive rankings among pharmacy employees
- **Communication**: To send important app updates, notifications, and support messages

### 3.2 Secondary Purposes
- **App Improvement**: To analyze usage patterns and improve app functionality
- **Security**: To detect and prevent fraudulent activities and protect user accounts
- **Compliance**: To meet legal and regulatory requirements
- **Analytics**: To understand user behavior and optimize user experience

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage Location
- Your personal information is stored securely on **Google Firebase Firestore** servers
- Authentication data is managed through **Firebase Authentication**
- Profile images and other media are stored on **Firebase Storage**

### 4.2 Security Measures
- **Encryption**: All data transmission uses SSL/TLS encryption
- **Authentication**: Secure email/password authentication with Firebase
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access to limit data exposure
- **Regular Security Updates**: We maintain up-to-date security protocols

### 4.3 Data Retention
- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active
- **Performance Data**: Retained for historical tracking and leaderboard purposes
- **Deleted Accounts**: Personal information is deleted within 30 days of account deletion

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### 5.2 Limited Sharing
We may share your information only in the following circumstances:
- **Within Your Organization**: Other employees at your pharmacy may see your leaderboard rankings and public achievements
- **Service Providers**: With Firebase (Google) for data storage and authentication services
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law, court order, or government regulation
- **Safety and Security**: To protect our users, prevent fraud, or ensure app security

### 5.3 Anonymous Data
We may share anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be linked to individual users for analytics and research purposes.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Account Control
- **Access**: You can view all your personal information through your profile
- **Update**: You can modify your profile information at any time
- **Delete**: You can request account deletion through the app or by contacting us
- **Export**: You can request a copy of your personal data

### 6.2 Permissions
- **Camera**: Required for barcode scanning and profile pictures. You can deny this permission, but some features will be unavailable.
- **Notifications**: Optional push notifications for updates and rewards. You can disable these in device settings.

### 6.3 Data Portability
You have the right to request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

## 7. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for use by adults who are pharmacy employees. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18 years of age. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information immediately.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. When we transfer your data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your privacy rights.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:
- Posting the updated policy in the app
- Sending email notifications for significant changes
- In-app notifications for major privacy updates
